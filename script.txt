Now let's move over to Cloud Storage. We have our chronicle-output bucket here. It has three folders. The clips folder holds the individual video clips produced by Veo 3.1 for each segment of the documentary. And the finals folder is where the completed documentary ends up — the final assembled video that gets delivered back to the user.
